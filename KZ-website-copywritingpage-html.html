COPYWRITING

As Communications Manager at Mozilla, I write words for websites, products, publications, and speeches. I also handle press, pitching, and media placements.

Here's a sampling of some recent work:

MOZILLA ADVOCACY: ENCRYPT

I led communications for Mozilla's Encrypt campaign, a public education initiative that taught everyday internet users about encryption's importance. I authored custom content, penned web copy, and assisted with script writing. The campaign reached millions of users and garnered coverage on NPR and CNN.



MOZILLA ADVOCACY: WEB FELLOWS

Mozilla's Open Web Fellowship places bright technologists at organizations like Amnesty International and the ACLU. Their mission: Help the world's leading NGOs keep the web open and free. I authored our Year 1 publication, an in-depth exploration of the program's success and impact. Read the report.



MOZILLA LEARNING: THIMBLE
